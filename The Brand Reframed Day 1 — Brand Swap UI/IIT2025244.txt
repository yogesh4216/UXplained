Brand Pair: YouTube → Netflix Style
Description: This design transforms YouTube into a Netflix-like experience with a cinematic hero banner, overlayed title, and call-to-action. Videos are displayed in horizontal carousels with hover previews, combining YouTube’s dynamic content with Netflix’s immersive layout and dark, cinematic theme.
